DEW x Call of Duty Modern Warfare II

To promote the release of Call of Duty Modern Warfare II, Activision teamed up with MTN DEW to create an in-game insentive when buying MTN DEW products. I had a blast developing the site to redeem your in-game points and rewards. From the imersive homepage to connecting the Activision API for redemption, this project taught me a lot on both the front and back end of site development.
